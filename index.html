ISHOWSPEED

Youtuber

IShowSpeed (Darren Jason Watkins Jr.), born January 21, 2005, is a premier American streamer and YouTube personality known for his high-energy, chaotic, and often comedic content. With over 50 million YouTube subscribers as of 2026, he is a global phenomenon renowned for his love of football (specifically Cristiano Ronaldo), athletic stunts, and international tours.
